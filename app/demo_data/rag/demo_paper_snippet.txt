Typical GaN DFT setup notes (example):
- Exchange-correlation: PBE (GGA)
- Plane-wave cutoff: 350-500 eV (often reported as ecut or PW cutoff)
- k-point mesh: e.g. 6x6x4 for wurtzite conventional cell, scaled for supercells
- Smearing: Fermi-Dirac, width ~0.1 eV
- SCF convergence: energy 1e-4 to 1e-3 eV, density ~1e-3 to 1e-2
- PAW setups / pseudopotentials: standard PAW; details vary by code
